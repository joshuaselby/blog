The Magic Wand

Jun 5, 2020 · 1 min read

Once, I had a magic wand, of it I was so very proud.

First I saw a bog, Bang! It turned into a dog.

Next, I saw a hat, Boom! It turned into a cat.

Next, I saw a pen, Whoosh! It turned into a hen.

Last, I saw a horrid house, Crash! It turned into a mouse.
